I’ve always been fascinated by woodwind instruments, particularly the flute and clarinet. The way the breath flows through these instruments and transforms into a beautiful sound is nothing short of magical. I began playing the flute in high school and quickly realized that it was more than just a hobby—it was a way for me to express myself deeply and personally. I’ve always been drawn to percussion instruments—there’s something about rhythm that’s pure and primal.
